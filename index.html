Our Story

Redefining Real Estate Excellence

FewFeets, powered by Zillion Realty, is a premium real estate advisory platform built for high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors seeking exceptional properties across continents.

Founded by Satvinder Singh Luthra, we believe that real estate at its finest is not just about square footage — it's about legacy, lifestyle, and strategic value. Every property in our portfolio is hand-selected, meticulously verified, and presented with the discretion our clients expect.

From luxury residences in India's most coveted addresses to commercial landmarks and international investment opportunities, FewFeets bridges the gap between ambition and acquisition.

Satvinder Singh Luthra Founder & Managing Director Zillion Realty
